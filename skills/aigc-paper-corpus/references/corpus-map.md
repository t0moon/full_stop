# AIGC Paper Corpus Map

## Scope

This corpus currently contains 22 PDFs. It is no longer only a Chinese AIGC media corpus. It now mixes:

- Chinese journal papers on AIGC, publishing, search, advertising, short video, and intelligent communication
- English journal papers on AI-generated advertising, disclosure effects, video-tool adoption, and human-versus-AI video comparison
- a small number of theses and project papers that are useful for gap-finding but should be treated more cautiously than peer-reviewed journal articles

Read this file first for orientation. Then use [paper-abstracts.md](paper-abstracts.md) for extracted abstracts, and open original PDFs when the task depends on precise design, measures, or source quality.

## Theme Clusters

### 1. Information Search, Trust, And User Cognition

- `AIGC多模态信息搜索用户认知特征实验研究`
  Experimental study of user cognition across image, video, and music search tasks. Useful for cognition variables, task familiarity, and multimodal search behavior.
- `AIGC技术对信息茧房效应的强化机制——基于情感偏向性的信息检索实证研究`
  Controlled comparison of Google, ChatGPT-4o, and Perplexity. Useful for emotional bias, information homogenization, and algorithmic amplification.
- `人智交互体验中算法素养对AIGC感知可信度的影响研究`
  Structural-model study of algorithm literacy and perceived credibility. Useful for trust, credibility, and user-side evaluation mechanisms.

### 2. Reading, Publishing, And Knowledge Communication

- `人机协同阅读视域下AIGC文本阐释异化的表征、机理与调适路径`
  Normative critique of interpretation alienation in human-machine reading.
- `从文本到视听：AIGC助力古诗古曲多模态阅读推广的技术路径研究`
  Text-image-video transformation for cultural communication. Useful for multimodal conversion logic.
- `AIGC时代的学术出版伦理：风险挑战与治理路径`
  Governance-oriented discussion of ethical risks in academic publishing.
- `AIGC赋能出版全产业链的内在逻辑与实践路径`
  Whole-chain view of publishing transformation under AIGC.
- `AIGC重塑网络文学IP：从线性写作到循环生成工作流研究`
  Useful for workflow transformation, narrative management, and editor-role redefinition.

### 3. Advertising Generation, Tool Adoption, And Industry Change

- `从大数据驱动到AI知识库生成：认知计算广告研究范式的变革`
  High-level paradigm paper. Useful for positioning AI advertising inside a broader computational-advertising shift.
- `基于生成式AI的合成广告：业态革新、风险隐忧与价值的再审视`
  Core paper for synthetic advertising, industry transformation, and value/risk discussion.
- `生成式AI广告的拟人化创作：基于隐喻的KTI模型研究`
  Core paper for AI ad creativity, anthropomorphism, metaphor, and acceptance.
- `Content Quality Over Functionality: How Aesthetics, Authenticity, and Creativity Drive Designers' Adoption of AI-Generated Video Advertising Tools`
  Strong bridge paper between content features and tool adoption. Especially useful for `aesthetics`, `authenticity`, `creativity`, and designer intention.
- `AIGC驱动短视频内容创作的全维变革与范式重构`
  Not ad-specific, but highly useful for full-process content production logic in video settings.

### 4. AI Advertising Effects, Disclosure, And Attitude Formation

- `Effect of disclosing AI-generated content on prosocial advertising evaluation`
  Directly relevant to disclosure effects, ad credibility, ad attitudes, and donation intention in prosocial advertising.
- `The effect of an AI-generated advertisement on consumer attitudes`
  Master's thesis on ad attitude and brand attitude using a video-ad stimulus. Especially useful because it explicitly notes that video-ad disclosure evidence is still limited.
- `The Impact of Artificial Intelligence in Advertising: An Experimental Evaluation of AI Video Generators`
  Thesis-level source on AI video generators in advertising. Use with caution, but it is highly relevant to AI advertising video as a stimulus and evaluation object.
- `人智交互体验中算法素养对AIGC感知可信度的影响研究`
  Use together with the ad papers when writing about credibility, trust, and acceptance mechanisms.

### 5. Human-Made Versus AI-Generated Content Comparison

- `Comparing human-made and AI-generated teaching videos: An experimental study on learning effects`
  Direct human-versus-AI video comparison. Not an ad study, but very useful for arguing that comparison designs already exist.
- `From recorded to AI-generated instructional videos: A comparison of learning performance and experience`
  Direct comparison between recorded and AI-generated videos. Useful for experience measures, retention, social presence, and perceived quality.
- `Comparing large Language models and human annotators in latent content analysis of sentiment, political leaning, emotional intensity and sarcasm`
  Text-side human-versus-AI comparison plus content-analysis methodology. Useful as a methodological analogue, not an advertising study.

### 6. Short Video, Audiovisual Production, And Platform Governance

- `行动者网络视域下AIGC重塑网络视听内容生产的机制与路径`
  Core paper on platforms, creators, users, and AIGC as a production infrastructure.
- `城市记忆短视频AIGC重构的伦理风险及其治理`
  Useful for value risks, locality, and governance in short-video production.
- `AIGC驱动短视频内容创作的全维变革与范式重构`
  Useful for workflow reconstruction and human-machine collaboration in video production.

### 7. Macro Review Of Intelligent Communication

- `术道之合：生成式AI的本体论、认识论与方法论——2023年以来中外学界智能传播研究的文献综述`
  High-level review that helps position narrower papers inside broader intelligent-communication debates.

## What The Enlarged Corpus Now Makes Possible

- Write a more credible literature review on `AI advertising video` rather than only `AIGC advertising` in general.
- Separate `application studies` from `effect studies` and from `human-versus-AI comparison studies`.
- Build a stronger research gap:
  many papers discuss how AI is used in advertising,
  several papers now discuss attitudes, disclosure, trust, or designer adoption,
  but ad-specific `AI video versus human-made video content comparison` remains thin.
- Connect advertising content questions with short-video production questions, instead of treating them as separate worlds.

## Source-Type Cautions

- Peer-reviewed journal articles in this corpus should usually carry more argumentative weight than theses.
- The theses are still useful when identifying recent research gaps, experimental setups, or stimulus design ideas.
- Some extracted metadata for downloaded English PDFs were curated manually because the filenames were noisy. Verify against the original PDF if you cite them formally.
- Auto-extracted abstracts and keywords are good for orientation, not for page-specific citation.

## Reusable Angles

- `tool application`: how AIGC is inserted into advertising or video workflows
- `content quality`: aesthetics, authenticity, creativity, anthropomorphism
- `effect evaluation`: credibility, ad attitude, brand attitude, donation intention, acceptance
- `comparison logic`: human-made versus AI-generated text/video
- `platform production`: creators, users, infrastructure, governance
- `research gap`: strong work on application and effect, weaker work on ad-video content structure comparison
